press ESC to pause the game.

PANEL SELECT:
left and right arrows to navigate selection
can also press numbers 0-9 to select that panel (0 is panel X)
select a panel with space

TRIVIA:
for most games:
double tap S to reveal the answer if there is any
double tap Y if the contestant is correct
double tap N if the contestant is wrong

LIGHTNING ROUND: 

once the lightning round has started, press left or right depending on how the contestant replies
once all questions are gone, the host must still use the Y or N buttons to procede, this allows them to override any mistakes made

MULTIPLE CHOICE: 
use directional keys to highlight an answer, and space to select that answer


AUDIO QUESTIONS:
pressing space again will restart the audio.
